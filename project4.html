Awesomenauts

Awesomenauts is a 2D sidescrolling Multiplayer Online Battle Arena game for Xbox Live Arcade, PlayStation Network and PC.

It's the year 3587, conflict spans the stars. Huge robot armies are locked in an enduring stalemate. In their bid for galactic conquest they call upon the most powerful group of mercenaries in the universe: the Awesomenauts!

Awesomenauts has sold over 4 million units across all platforms and has been met with critical acclaim upon it's releases on multiple platforms. Metacritic score 78.

This was the longest project I have worked on, the game received many patches, updates and expansions. Went through a successful Kickstarter for an expansion and went free to play in 2017.
