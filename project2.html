Snowball Earth

Snowball Earth is an unreleased game by Ronimo Games and the first project the team at Ronimo Games worked on.

As a small robot you control a set of robot dogs who emit heat. With motion controls you can throw and roll the dogs to certain areas to melt snow and ice and unfreeze a frozen world. I created puzzles and challenges within the levels and the core gameplay design.
