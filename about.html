Fabian Akker

Co-founder, business development manager and lead game designer at Ronimo Games

Hi! I’m a developer with over 15 years of experience in the game industry. In 2007 I started Ronimo together with 6 others and currently the studio has grown to 30 developers.

My day-to-day work at Ronimo has always been split between working on the games as a lead designer, directing the studio and managing all business affairs for the company. The latest game I have worked on is Blightbound, a game published by Devolver, which will be released later this year.

I’m looking for new opportunities and you can contact me at fabianakker@gmail.com.
