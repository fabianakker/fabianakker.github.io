Swords & Soldiers

Swords & Soldiers is a side scrolling real time strategy game. Released on Nintendo Switch, WiiU, PlayStation Network, WiiWare, PC, Mac, Android, iPad, iPhone and iPod Touch.

Rewrite history as you take control of the brutal Vikings, the devious Aztecs or the crafty Chinese in their global quest to win the favour of the Gods!

Swords & Soldiers has sold over 500.000 units across all platforms and has been met with critical acclaim with a Metacritic score of 84. I was lead designer on the project and had many different tasks such as creating the story, levels, units, balancing, onboarding, dialog and testing.
